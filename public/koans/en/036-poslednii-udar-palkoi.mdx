# Koan №36 — "The Last Strike of the Stick"

<Action>Recorded nowhere. Found by no one. The master did not dictate this koan — he was silent. The student wrote down the silence. The master read the record and said: "Almost."</Action>

<Action>The student came one day without a question.</Action>

<Action>The master waited for a question. The student was silent. The master waited longer. The student looked at the garden.</Action>

<Action>The master raised the stick.</Action>

<Action>The student did not turn away.</Action>

<Action>The master lowered the stick.</Action>

<Master>"You have stopped asking."</Master>

<Student>"I have stopped waiting for an answer."</Student>

<Action>The master rose and went into the room. A minute later he returned with tea — for the student.</Action>

<Haiku>The garden. Silence. The stick is lowered. The tea — is hot.</Haiku>

If you cannot explain JavaScript to yourself — you do not know it. If you can — you no longer need a master.</Question>
